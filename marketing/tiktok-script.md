## General tips for filming:
1. Use screen recording (Windows: Win+G, Mac: Cmd+Shift+5)
2. Show the actual app, not just talking
3. Keep videos under 30 seconds for better reach
4. Post at peak hours (7-9 AM or 7-10 PM your local time)
5. Use trending sounds if they fit
6. Reply to comments with more demo videos
7. Post consistently (3-5 times per week)
